About Zefs Guides

Zefs Guides are little books and learning materials about machine learning and data science related topics.


They are designed to help job seekers, students, and working professionals gain a stronger understanding of the concepts behind the latest ML and data science techniques.


The first Zefs Guides project is a four book series about Deep Learning, which will be available in paperback and ebook formats. There will also be sold-separately digital flashcards to accompany each book.
